Flight Lieutenant Gunjan Saxena (born 1975) is an Indian Air Force (IAF) officer and former helicopter pilot. She joined the IAF in 1994 and is a 1999 Kargil War veteran. She is the only woman to be part of the Kargil War, making her the first woman IAF officer to go to war.

Cast : Ishan kattar, Tabu, Danish razvi, Rasika Dugal, tanya maniktala

Director : Sharan Sharma

Genre : Serious, drama, historic
